Honest answers

Questions you'd actually ask.

Is this a subscription? +

No. One-time payment. Lifetime access. No recurring charges, no auto-renewal, no "free trial" that secretly bills you. Pay once, log in forever.

Why are there 4 tiers if all 3 brands are included in every one? +

Tiers limit the number of activities visible, not the modules. Starter ($27) gives you 250 activities across all 4 brands — enough to confirm the format works for you. Pro ($97) gives 750. Elite ($197) gives 1,500+ plus UnboxED and resource PDFs. School ($497) unlocks all 2,515 plus 10 educator seats. ParentED (550+ home strategies) is included free with every tier. Most users start at Pro and upgrade later if they want everything.

Can I see the dashboard before I buy? +

Yes. The live dashboard has a one-click demo button for each tier — no signup, no card. Click around, switch brands, open activity cards, see exactly what you're paying for. The activity card on this page is the real component, randomly pulled from the catalog.

Who is this actually for? +

AdaptED: early childhood educators, homeschool families, child care providers, YMCA program leaders, childcare workers. CampED: summer camp counselors, after-school program leaders, scout troop leaders, youth group volunteers. TeenagED: middle school teachers, parents of pre-teens, life-skills educators, mentors. RegulatED: educators and parents focused on emotional regulation and social-emotional development. ParentED: any parent or caregiver who wants practical home strategies backed by 20 years of early childhood experience.

Are these activities actually original? +

170 are hand-curated and original — those are the seed activities written by educators with classroom and camp experience. The remaining ~2,300 are systematic variants of those seeds, generated to cover every age range and group size combination. Think of it like a textbook with 170 chapters and per-chapter exercises. The ideas are real; the variations multiply the use cases.

Will there be more updates? +

Yes. New modules in the roadmap — HomeEd, SportsEd, SoothED (mental health), CalmED. New activities added to existing modules monthly. All updates are free for current license holders. You'll get an email when each ships.
