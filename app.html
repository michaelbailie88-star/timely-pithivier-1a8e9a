About This Vault

🎯 What this is A unified activity vault for educators, camp counselors, parents, and mentors. Five modules in one license — AdaptED, CampED, TeenagED, RegulatED, and ParentED. Every activity has a goal, conversation starter, educator tip, materials list, and group-size adaptations. One-time payment. Lifetime access. No subscription.
👤 Who made it Made by Tatianna Bailie and Michael — The Lazy Creator Co. Tatianna is a over 20 years working with children and youth of all ages & over 15 years with the YMCA. She spent years searching for quality activities and got tired of the hunt. Michael built the platform so she never has to open another Pinterest tab.

What's inside

  • 2,700+ activities across AdaptED, CampED, TeenagED, and RegulatED
  • 550+ ParentED strategies — home-based parenting strategies across 10 topics, each fully printable
  • UnboxED tab: 575+ cards built around 12 everyday materials
  • RegulatED Tool Kit: 4 interactive, printable SEL tools — Active Listening Guide, Problem Solving Wheel, Body Calming Toolkit, and Zones of Regulation
  • Resources tab: Printable planning PDFs and communication boards by Tatianna Bailie
  • Planner: Save and organize activities into weekly session plans
  • Every activity includes: goal, conversation starter, educator tip, steps, materials, and small + large group adaptations

The five modules

  • 📘 AdaptED — Early childhood educators, homeschool families, child care providers, YMCA programs. Ages 0–12. Sensory play, fine motor, social-emotional, developmental skill-building. Multi-color themed.
  • ⛺ CampED — Summer camps, after-school, scout troops. Ages 6–18. Cabin activities, wide games, camp songs, survival skills, ceremonies. Deep forest green.
  • 🎒 TeenagED — Life-prep for ages 11–15. Mentors, parents, life-skills teachers. Chores, cooking, money, communication, transit, social skills. Deep indigo.
  • 🧘 RegulatED — Social-emotional learning for ages 2–12. Emotional regulation, sensory strategies, co-regulation activities, impulse control games. Deep rust.
  • 🏠 ParentED — Real strategies for home, by Tatianna Bailie. Big feelings, routines, screen time, independence, sensory play, siblings, mealtime, and more. 550+ tap-to-open cards with print support.
